Hamptons Spotless Service

Logo Refinement & Landing Page Proposal

Overview

Hamptons Spotless Service is a new cleaning business launching in the Hamptons, New York. The business has an existing logo concept that requires refinement, and needs a professional static website to establish its online presence.

The purpose of this engagement is to finalise the logo and deliver a clean, credible landing page that positions the business appropriately from day one, with the flexibility to build out additional functionality over time.

This project is delivered as a single, cohesive engagement covering logo refinement and website design and build, to ensure visual consistency and a strong first impression across all touchpoints.

Delivery Approach

The project is delivered sequentially to reduce risk and maintain clarity:

  1. Refine and finalise the logo
  2. Establish the visual direction for the website
  3. Design and build the landing page
  4. Review, refine, and launch

This approach ensures the logo is locked in before the website is designed around it, resulting in a cohesive and professional outcome.

Review & Feedback

Each phase of the project includes structured review and feedback to ensure alignment before progressing.

  • Feedback is consolidated and provided in a single round per phase
  • Revisions focus on refinement and alignment, not re-direction
  • Each phase is signed off before moving to the next to maintain timeline and momentum

This approach keeps the project efficient while ensuring decisions are considered and intentional.

Scope of Work

1. Logo Refinement

Review and refinement of the existing logo concept to ensure it communicates the right level of professionalism and trust for the Hamptons market.

This includes:

  • Assessment of the current logo and identification of areas for improvement
  • Presentation of 2â€“3 refined variations for review
  • One round of refinement on the selected direction
  • Final logo delivered in multiple formats:
    • SVG (scalable, web-ready)
    • PNG (transparent background, multiple sizes)
    • Print-ready files

The goal is to retain the intent of the original concept while elevating its execution to a professional standard.

2. Landing Page Design & Build

Design and development of a static, single-page website that establishes credibility and drives enquiries.

The landing page will include:

  • Hero section with clear value proposition
  • Services overview
  • About / trust-building section
  • Testimonials (placeholder content if not yet available)
  • Contact section with form or call-to-action
  • Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile

Design direction will reference lazysusanscleaning.com as a benchmark for structure and conversion focus, adapted to suit the Hamptons market and positioning.

Design Approach

The website will be built as a static site, optimised for speed and simplicity, with a clean structure that supports future expansion.

  • Clean, professional aesthetic appropriate for the Hamptons market
  • Trust-building elements throughout (credentials, social proof)
  • Clear calls-to-action for booking or enquiries
  • SEO-optimised HTML structure
  • Built with future scalability in mind
