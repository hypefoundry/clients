How It Works

Four steps. One hire that lasts.

01

Brief & Scope

We learn your business, culture, and the kind of person who thrives in your team. Not a job spec — a hiring strategy.

02

Search & Shortlist

Network first, not job boards. Pre-qualified candidates — most not actively looking — matched to your brief within 48 hours.
