Common Questions

Before you submit. The answers you need.

What does it cost?
Nothing upfront. Virtus works on contingency — you only pay when we place someone who starts. No retainers, no lock-in contracts.
How quickly can you fill a role?
48 hours from brief to shortlist. Most permanent roles are filled within 2–4 weeks. We send two or three candidates, not twenty.
What sectors do you cover?
Accounting & Finance, Technology & IT, Legal, Human Resources, and Marketing. Each consultant recruits exclusively in one sector.
Do you handle contract and temporary roles?
Yes. Project work, parental cover, peak periods — we place contract and temporary professionals across all five sectors.
What makes Virtus different from other agencies?
Depth, not breadth. Five sectors, not fifty. Every consultant recruits exclusively in one area — they know the salary bands, the hiring managers, and the candidates. That is why 92% of our placements are still in role at 12 months.
