Same people. Same track record.

Virtus is the white-collar recruitment division of Momentum Consulting. Built on twelve years of placing professionals who stay, now with a brand that matches the calibre of the people we place.

Our Story

Built inside Momentum Consulting

Momentum Consulting has spent twelve years earning a reputation for recruitment that delivers. Reliable placements. Long-term relationships. People who stay.
