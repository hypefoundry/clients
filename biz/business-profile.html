Overview

What the business does

A mobile preventative health service that sends a registered veterinarian to pet owners' homes to conduct comprehensive health assessments — including 60+ biomarker blood panels, physical examinations, and urine/stool analysis — then delivers a personalised health report with ongoing care recommendations.

Core proposition: Catch health problems in dogs and cats before symptoms appear — saving pets' lives and owners thousands in emergency treatment costs.

Operations

How it runs

Team
Owner (business, marketing, digital) + 1 contracted registered SA veterinarian
Scheduling
Online booking via Calendly, confirmed by email. Morning, afternoon, and weekend slots.
Lab partner
Accredited veterinary pathology lab (IDEXX / Vetpath). Samples collected in-home, couriered same day.
Reports
Personalised health report generated from lab results + vet notes. Delivered digitally within 72 hours.
Follow-up
Telehealth video call with the vet to walk through results and care plan. Included in base price.
Technology
Custom client app (health dashboard, pet profiles, results history). Built and maintained by owner.
Annual costs
Insurance $2,000 + miscellaneous $2,400 = $4,400 fixed annually

Go-to-Market

Launch strategy

  • 🧪 Phase 0 — Validate ($900): Run a Facebook/Instagram ad campaign to a landing page in eastern suburbs. Target: 10 bookings to prove demand before committing.
  • 🤝 Phase 1 — Vet partner: Secure a contracted SA-registered veterinarian. Priority targets: Adelaide Home Vet, Pet Doctor Vet — both have service overlap and geographic fit.
  • 🏠 Phase 2 — Foundation (months 1–3): Launch with 6–8 visits per week in Burnside, Unley, Norwood. Referral program, local vet partnerships, pet-friendly venue presence.
  • 📈 Phase 3 — Expansion (months 4–8): Grow to 10–12 visits per week. Add Annual Wellness Membership. SEO, Google Ads, social content.
  • 🚀 Phase 4 — Growth (months 9–12): Expand service area. Second vet contractor if demand warrants. Target: 16 visits/week by end of Year 1.
Key Risks

Risks & mitigations

Risk Mitigation
Vet recruitment Approach mobile/locum vets already set up for home visits. Offer flexible schedule + competitive per-visit rate.
Low initial demand Validate with $900 MVP ad test before committing startup costs. Pivot or kill fast.
Price sensitivity $650 is within the $400–$800 range Australians already spend annually on pet care. Frame as prevention vs $5K–$15K emergency.
Regulatory All vet work performed by registered SA practitioners. Business does not provide veterinary advice — it facilitates access.
Hugo enters Adelaide Hugo is dogs-only, VC-funded, and focused on Sydney/Melbourne. Adelaide market is too small for their model. Local trust is the moat.
