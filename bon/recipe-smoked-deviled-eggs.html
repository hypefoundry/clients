Appetiser

Smoked
Deviled Eggs

Elevate your next party or gathering with Smoked Deviled Eggs. This classic appetizer gets a delicious upgrade with a hint of smoky flavor, making it a surefire hit with your guests.

Prep Time 20 min
Smoke Time 5 min
Serves 12
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

Deviled eggs
  • Large eggs6
  • Mayonnaise3 tbsp
  • Dijon mustard1 tsp
  • White vinegar1 tsp
  • Salt and pepperTo taste
  • Paprika, for garnish
For smoking
  • Wood chips (apple or cherry)1 pinch
  • Bon Apperoo™ Smoker1

Method

01

Boil eggs

Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat, cover, and let sit for 12 minutes. Transfer to an ice water bath to cool completely.

02

Peel and halve

Peel the cooled eggs and halve them lengthwise. Carefully remove the yolks into a mixing bowl, keeping the whites intact.

03

Prepare filling

Mash the yolks with a fork, then mix with mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, white vinegar, salt, and pepper until smooth and creamy.

04

Fill whites

Spoon or pipe the yolk mixture back into the egg white halves. For a polished presentation, use a piping bag with a star tip.

05

Add smoke

Use a Bon Apperoo™ smoker with apple or cherry wood chips to infuse the deviled eggs with smoke for about 5 minutes. The smoke will settle into the creamy filling, adding a beautiful depth of flavour.

06

Garnish and serve

Sprinkle with paprika and serve immediately while the smoky aroma is still fresh. For extra flair, add a small sprig of dill to each egg.

Pro Tip

Fold in chopped chives and crispy bacon bits for a savoury, textured filling — or add a dash of smoked paprika to the yolk mixture itself. The paprika deepens the smoke flavour so it carries through every bite, not just the finish.

Pairs well with

Smoked Old Fashioned

A smoky bourbon cocktail is the natural companion — the shared smoke character ties the appetiser and drink together beautifully.

Garden Salad

A light, crisp salad with lemon vinaigrette cuts through the richness of the eggs and balances the smoky depth.

Charcuterie Board

Cured meats, pickles, and crusty bread — the perfect spread to serve alongside smoked deviled eggs at any gathering.
